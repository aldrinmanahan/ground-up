Our Story:

Built from the Ground Up.

We believe the best things take time and dedication. From sourcing sustainable beans to mastering the perfect extraction, we've built our craft from the ground up to ensure every sip is better than the last.

Local Roast. Fresh Brew.

Founded in 2025, Ground Up was born from a simple belief: a great cup of coffee does more than wake you up—it fuels the connections that move our city forward. Located in the heart of Katipunan, we bridge the gap between the farm and your daily ritual.

The Craft: We treat roasting as a science and brewing as an art, using precision extraction to reveal the hidden notes in every bean.

The Source: We partner with farmers who prioritize regenerative soil health, ensuring every sip supports a sustainable future.

The Community: Our space is built for the dreamers—the students, the artists, and the thinkers of QC.

The Perfect Swirl: Where our bold, precision-roasted espresso meets creamy, cold perfection.

Our Mission

To elevate the daily coffee ritual through world-class roasting and a community-first atmosphere. We believe every cup is a masterpiece—crafted with precision, served with warmth, and sourced with integrity.

Our Vision

To become the heartbeat of the neighborhood, where the pursuit of the perfect roast inspires connection, creativity, and a higher standard for specialty coffee. We strive to be the place where every guest feels at home and every cup excels.
