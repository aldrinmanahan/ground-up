Great Coffee, From the Ground Up®

Experience coffee at its peak. We source the highest-grade, ethically-grown beans to ensure every cup is a masterpiece of flavor and sustainability. Ground Up is more than a coffee shop—it's a commitment to the art of the perfect brew.

Whether it's a precision-poured single-origin roast, a handcrafted espresso, or a fresh-baked pastry, we bring old-world dedication to every sip. Your morning deserves nothing less.
